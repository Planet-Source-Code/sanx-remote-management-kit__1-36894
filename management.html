Once you click any of the buttons, your machine may appear to freeze for up to ten seconds or so. This is pefectly normal and should be expected.

Why isn't it working?

For this system to work, the following conditions have to be met:
  • The client and target machines have to be running Windows 2000 or above.
  • You must have admin rights on both the client and target machines.
  • You must have the Initialize and script ActiveX controls not marked as safe option in Internet Explorer enabled.
